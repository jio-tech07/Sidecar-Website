What We Do

Professional Fabrication Services

From heavy-duty sidecar builds to precision welding, JMR delivers top-tier metalwork tailored to your specific needs.

Custom Sidecar Builds

Bespoke passenger and utility sidecars custom-fit to any motorcycle model. Sturdy tubing, comfortable alignment, and premium suspension.

Repairs & Reinforcement

Complete structural diagnostics and welding repairs for worn, cracked, or bent motorcycle frames, sidecar hitches, and alignment adjusters.

Precision Welding

Expert TIG/MIG and ARC welding for stainless steel and galvanized steel. Guaranteed robust penetration and clean finishes.

Custom Metal Fabrication

Brackets, cargo racks, safety guards, and customized utility attachments built according to your technical blueprints or drawings.
